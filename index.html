War of the Realms Explodes Across the Universe with 'Spider-Man & The League of Realms'

This May, Spidey teams-up with an unlikely crew in the new limited series!

WRITTEN BY TUCKER CHET MARKUS

The cosmic calamity of WAR OF THE REALMS will shock every hero in the Marvel Universe into action—and that includes your friendly neighborhood Spider-Man! This May, the wallcrawler responds to the siege of Midgard by joining an unlikely team in WAR OF THE REALMS: SPIDER-MAN & THE LEAGUE OF REALMS!

Written by Sean Ryan with art by Nico Leon and covers by Ken Lashley, the new three-issue limited series sees Spidey on an unprecedented adventure in a crucial battle that just might change the course of the Universe-wide saga...

The ragtag group has assembled...but who will represent Asgard? And since Spidey is such a terrible leader, does this cross-realm team-up have any chance at survival, let alone success?!
